~ Bespoke web design and development for the wildest dreamers ~

Here at Verdi Studio, we are first and foremost: storytellers. There's nothing we love more than swapping tales over a glass of something sweet (usually a hot chocolate, but sometimes a cocktail).

That's why we're passionate about partnering with small businesses and creatives to help weave the threads of their tale together into thoughtfully designed custom-coded websites. Just like you, they're one of a kind.

Let's create something beautiful

We believe in giving our full attention, which is why we enjoy taking on projects one at a time and delivering our clients their website within 3 weeks. It's as easy as 1,2,3!

01

02

This is what we refer to as the collaboration stage, where you will work with us to pull together all the information and materials we need for the real magic to happen next.

03

The most exciting part ~ designing and building your website! In 3 weeks we'll go from moodboards and wireframes to a fully custom-coded website, all set up and ready to go.
