Asanas

Asanas is a wellness brand focused on using holistic methods to make healthy choices. They are full of positive and welcoming vibes, and wanted a new website to showcase their vast range of services.

They already had a bunch of imagery from past events that they wanted to use, which set the tone for the entire website. Julie wanted a soft, somewhat neutral colour palette and something to really encompass their values.



Keywords:

organic, soft, inviting, warm
