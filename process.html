The Process

Our clients deserve our full, undivided attention; which is why Verdi Studio focuses on just one project at a time within a three-week-build.

We know first hand that your own business is an extension of your heart, soul and passion. That's why we see this as a collaborative experience and include our clients in our work every single step of the way.



It all starts with a story...

If you're curious about working with us, all we ask for is a simple email enquiry from you, explaining a little about your business and what kind of website you're looking for. You can even send us design inspiration, if you'd like! Based on this we can send you an initial quote, and if we choose to go forward from here: the real fun begins!

THREE WEEKS TO CREATE SOME MAGIC

Once we've agreed to work together, our clients use our Google Calendar to book their three-week-build slot. We work together beforehand on prep work, such as collaborating on moodboards and design inspirations, but it's during this time slot that the magic happens.

During our first week together, Verdi Studio creates the final designs and lock in wireframes for all pages of your site. Some pre-build code will be happening behind the scenes whilst you take a look over and approve the wireframes.

A busy week for Verdi Studio, as we turning the approved designs into a fully working website! You'll recieve sneak peeks along the way, with a big reveal just before the weekend begins!

We like to refer to this as “finishing touches” week. After you've looked over your website, we'll implement edits along the way before handing over the keys at the end of the week. Now we celebrate!

We create long-lasting designs that make a impact.

When it comes to design, we are inspired by those around us; whether it be people, architecture or the natural world. We lean towards timeless, understated designs but have been known to dabble in the unexpected too.

We recognise that our websites have one main purpose: which is to showcase your business and to tell your stories. Therefore, as long as we create something that truly reflects who you are, we'll feel like we've done our job.





FAQ

How much do you quote for a website?

Every project is unique so we like to keep our prices as flexible as we can in order to meet our client's individual needs. To keep things simple, we classify projects as small, medium and large. Small being a simple, one page website and large being a 5-6+ page site with a blog or shop. Our prices can range anywhere from £700 - £2,200+. For a more bespoke quote, please get in touch by emailing a little bit about what you're looking for and we'll tailor it to your needs.

What's included in a package?

Each and every website we create is fully custom-coded to our client's specific needs, but all of our packages include:

  • Client Welcome Pack with results of questionnaire and contract
  • Page wireframe designs and moodboards ahead of the website build.
  • Website designed with UI/UX principles (eg. mobile friendy) and with accessibility in mind (eg. colour contrasting checks)
  • A tutorial on how you can make simple changes and edits to your site using the Content Management System
  • Ongoing support from Verdi Studio

    • Who is responsible for the website after it's built?

We always hand over our websites with a tutorial on how to use the CMS. However, some clients prefer for us to do so instead; which we are happy to do for small fee.
