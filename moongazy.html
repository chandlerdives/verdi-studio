Moongazy Jewellery

An ethically sourced jewellery brand established in 2022. When the owener of Moongazy Jewellery reached out to us to design her new website she had a clear vision of what she wanted: something beautiful and luxurious with a little hint of mystery.

Moongazy Jewellery provided us with a strong set of brand photos from a recent photoshoot, which we were able to utilise throughout the site to really show off their beautiful brand. We chose an elegant boho font to create a timeless feel to match their brand ethos.



Keywords:

timeless, elegant, rich colours, sustainable
