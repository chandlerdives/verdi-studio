Crafted with passion, built foryour story

Chandler's story began in the world of marketing and PR, but a love for weaving narratives and the collaborative spirit of small teams soon led her to the world of web design. A life-altering adventure exploring Australia in a trusty VW Caravelle became the catalyst for honing her coding skills. It was there, amidst the vast landscapes, that the seed of The Verdi Collective was sown.

The Verdi Collective, a name echoing the spirit of creativity and collaboration, is a female-led haven where we specialize in crafting beautiful and strategic websites for independent and creative businesses like yours.

We are dreamers, storytellers and adventurers at heart who simply adore connecting with folks who share the fire - the passion for something truly special. That's why we enjoy projects where we collaborate closely, ensuring your website becomes a vibrant tapestry woven with your values and aspirations.

Speaking of journeys, where is Chandler now, you ask? Well, she's currently traversing Europe in a self-converted campervan (bigger than the last one!) - always on the search for new experiences! But fear not, her passion for listening and that infectious enthusiasm for your fiery ideas remain firmly in tow.
