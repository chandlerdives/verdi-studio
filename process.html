Let's go from vision to reality...

Wondering how it works? We begin each project with a deep dive into your goals and preferences. This allows us to tailor a custom quote that perfectly aligns with your needs. Before finalising anything, we ensure a perfect fit by confirming our studio's capabilities match your project's requirements.

I. Discovery

Your website journey starts with a conversation! After receiving your initial inquiry and understanding your preferred package, we'll schedule a video call to get to know each other better. This Discovery Call is your chance to share your vision, ask questions, and gain valuable insights into our design process. We'll work collaboratively to ensure your website aligns perfectly with your goals and brand identity.

II. Proposal

Once we have a clear understanding of your project needs and chosen package, we'll craft a personalised design proposal outlining the project scope, timeline and a detailed cost breakdown to help you to make an informed decision. Upon your approval, we'll provide an onboarding booklet which details communication channels, milestones, and next steps, keeping you informed and involved every step of the way.

III. Development

This is where your website takes shape! We'll work collaboratively throughout the development phase, which may involve design iterations, content gathering, and technical implementation specific to your chosen package. We'll keep you updated on progress to ensure the final product exceeds your expectations. Open communication is essential for crafting a website that truly reflects your unique brand and goals.

IIII. Delivery

The moment you've been waiting for! Once the website is meticulously tested and polished to perfection, we'll present it to you for final approval. Upon your green light, we'll launch your website, making it accessible to the world! Our commitment doesn't end there. We'll provide any necessary post-launch support to ensure a smooth transition and help you navigate the exciting world of online presence.
