Aroux Photography

After a chance visit as a junior PR to Africa, Alessia launched a career as a photographer focusing on humanitarian work, wildlife and conservation. She is passionate about forging connections through creative mediums and travels the world looking for stories within nature.

Aroux needed a online home to showcase her digital portfolio. She wanted something with a simple but impactful colour palette and a site that would allow her beautiful photographs to make an imapct. It had to be something she could send to prospective clients, to catch their attention.



Keywords:

Feminine, playful, bold, clean
